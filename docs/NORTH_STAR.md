# 1. Our Why

## The Problem

Stroke is one of the leading causes of long-term disability worldwide.

After leaving the hospital, recovery doesn't stop. In fact, most rehabilitation happens at home, away from therapists and structured clinical supervision.

This creates two major problems:

### For Patients

Patients often don't know:

- if they're improving
- whether they're doing exercises correctly
- whether today's rehabilitation is actually helping
- how close they are to getting their life back

Many lose motivation because progress feels invisible.

---

### For Therapists

Therapists only see patients during scheduled appointments.

Between visits they cannot easily understand:

- whether rehabilitation is being completed
- pain and fatigue experienced at home
- barriers preventing rehabilitation
- meaningful functional recovery
- who requires additional support

Clinical decisions are therefore made using limited information.

---

## Our Opportunity

Home rehabilitation should feel connected, meaningful and visible.

Patients deserve confidence.

Therapists deserve better insight.

---

# 2. Our Vision

Small steps today create meaningful recovery tomorrow.

We believe rehabilitation should not simply count completed exercises.

It should help patients regain the life they want to return to.

---

# 3. What is LinkCare?

LinkCare is a goal-oriented stroke rehabilitation platform.

It combines:

- A Patient App that guides home rehabilitation.
- A Therapist Dashboard that monitors recovery between appointments.

Together they create a continuous rehabilitation journey rather than isolated clinic visits.

---

# 4. Our Mission

Enable stroke survivors to recover confidently at home while giving therapists meaningful insight into recovery between appointments.

---

# 5. Our North Star

Everything we build should answer one question:

> "Does this help patients recover more confidently and help therapists make better decisions?"

If the answer is no, it does not belong in the MVP.

---

# 6. Design Philosophy

Every product decision follows these principles.

## Recovery feels hopeful

We celebrate progress.

We never punish inconsistency.

---

## Goals before exercises

Patients don't exercise for repetitions.

They exercise to:

- return to work
- regain independence
- cook again
- participate in family life
- reconnect with their community

Every rehabilitation session should connect back to meaningful life goals.

---

## Reduce cognitive load

Stroke survivors may experience fatigue, cognitive impairment and visual challenges.

The product should feel calm, simple and approachable.

---

## Clinical credibility

The product should feel trustworthy enough for therapists while remaining welcoming for patients.

# 9. Signature Features

These are LinkCare's defining features.

## Recovery Snapshot

Visual overview of upper limb recovery by body part.

---

## Goal Journey

Every rehabilitation session contributes toward meaningful life goals rather than arbitrary scores.

---

## Today's Focus

Patients always know what they should work on today.

---

## Session Capture

After every rehabilitation session patients complete a quick recovery check-in.

This captures:

- Pain
- Fatigue
- Difficulty
- Barriers
- Voice or text notes
- Confidence towards recovery goals

---

## Recovery Journal

A positive reflection of rehabilitation history.

Designed to encourage consistency rather than punish missed sessions.

---

## Weekly Consistency

A simple measure of rehabilitation engagement over time.

---

## Attention Status

Helps therapists quickly identify patients who may require additional support.
